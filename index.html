Biography

Youra Lee was working primarily in sculpture, but often incorporating fine art and installation art in her artwork. She transferred school from University of Seoul in Korea to Stony Brook University in NY in 2015 and changed her major from sculpture to studio art. It was a big turning point in her life and her artwork. She was studying photography and digital design intensively. She is now working to fuse many areas of arts. She is creative and unique, and her idea never stops. She is not only a designer, but also an artist. She uses 'aurora' as her disigner nickname to show her various color with brilliance like an aurora. Through her many exhibitions, she captures many eyes and breathes in her works. She always wants to communicate with people through her artworks and coexist with them in this world, under the name of creative designer. Youra mentions that designer is the best friend of people as interacting each other indirectly but sincerely.
